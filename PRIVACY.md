# Privacy Policy

Hoshi Reader does not collect any data. All your data stays on your device.

If you use the optional Google Drive sync feature, the app accesses only files that were created by the Google Cloud project. It cannot read any other files in your Drive. You can sign out at any time to remove all stored authentication tokens.

No analytics, tracking, or advertising is used.

For questions, open an issue on [GitHub](https://github.com/Manhhao/Hoshi-Reader/issues).
